Sparkr Media

What Is Sparkr?

Sparkr Media is a fictional company (at least for now) designed by aspiring software
engineers and activists Paul & Aston Martinez.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform in which we can spark meaningful, enlightening, and
educational conversations surrounding human rights issues in today's ever-changing
sociological landscape. We intend to do this for a wide range of topics using articles,
public speaking, and community forums.

Topics We'll Cover

  • Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

  • LGBTQ+

  • Ethnicity

  • Disabilities & Chronic Illness

  • ...and many more!
